A Bowl Of Cereal A Bowl Of Cereal

Description

A healthy start to the day

Ingredients

  • a bowl
  • your favourite cereal
  • milk
  • toppings (optional)
  • a spoon

Steps

  1. Grab your empty bowl
  2. fill with your cereal of choice
  3. add milk
  4. add toppings e.g. sugar, honey, etc
  5. eat with spoon and enjoy
