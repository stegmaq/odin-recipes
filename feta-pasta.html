Baked Feta Pasta

A dish Elly found on TikTok and has become a regular dinner

  • Feta
  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Chorizo
  • Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 Onion

Steps

  1. Place the feta in the centre of an oven proof dish
  2. surround the feta with whole baby plum tomatoes
  3. Slice the onion and chorizo and layer on top of the tomatoes
  4. add garlic, salt and pepper to flavour
  5. bake
  6. boil pasta
  7. once cooked remove from oven and drain the pasta
  8. mix together and serve with garlic bread
